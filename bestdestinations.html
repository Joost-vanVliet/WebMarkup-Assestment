Rome truly is the place to be if you want to be in a city that is not only rich in culture but also rich in the amount of ice-cream vendors, Truly a city like no other.

Stockholm

Stockholm is the capital of Sweden and a fascinating city. After the success of the video game “”Minecraft” the Swedish government decided that Sweden should look like the game, for this reason the entirety of Sweden is build with 1 by 1 meter cubes. This makes Sweden an unforgettable destination that will leave you confused and awestruck at the sight of a land made of blocks. This is real, everyone who says it is not is lying.
